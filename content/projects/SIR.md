COVID-19 pandemic has motivated developers around the world to offer projects and solutions that try to predict the evolution of the pandemic in the following weeks, months or even years. Probably the simplest model someone can use to model an epidemic is the SIR model. SIR.js is a JS library implementing the SIR epidemic simulation model.
