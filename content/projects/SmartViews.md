This project explores the use of a blockchain as an immutable ledger for storing historical facts in a decentralised Data Warehouse deployment. It also proposes a novel modular architecture in which aggregate data cube computations defined by smart contracts are passed to suitable data processing engines.
Supervisor: Prof. Yannis Kotidis.
The [paper of this thesis](http://pages.cs.aueb.gr/~kotidis/Publications/DaWakWeb.pdf) is published in “Proceedings of the 23rd International Conference on Big Data Analytics and Knowledge Discovery (DaWaK 2021)”.
